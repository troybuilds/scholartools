Is a 3.5 GPA Good?

A 3.5 GPA is considered a strong GPA and represents mostly A- and B+ grades.

Is a 3.5 GPA Good for College?

A 3.5 GPA can qualify you for many colleges and universities, especially state schools and community colleges.

Is a 3.5 GPA Good for Scholarships?

Some scholarships require a minimum GPA of 3.0, though more competitive scholarships may require higher GPAs.

How Much Will a 70 Affect My Grade?

A 70 can lower your grade, especially if the assignment has a high weight. The impact depends on your current grade and how much the assignment counts.

🎯 Example

  • Your current grade: 90%
  • The assignment is worth: 20%
  • You score: 70%

After this score, your grade would drop to about 86%.

📉 Why It Drops

Even though a 70 is a passing grade, it is still below an A or B average, so it can bring your overall grade down depending on the weight of the assignment.

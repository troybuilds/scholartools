How Final Grades Are Calculated

Most courses calculate final grades using weighted averages. Each category in the class contributes a percentage toward your final grade.

For example:

  • Homework: 20%
  • Quizzes: 20%
  • Midterm Exam: 30%
  • Final Exam: 30%

In this example, the final exam has a large impact on the overall course grade.

Why Students Use Final Grade Calculators

Students often want to know:

  • What grade they need on their final exam
  • If they can still reach a certain letter grade
  • How much the final exam will affect their grade

Using a calculator makes it easy to quickly determine the score required to reach your target grade.

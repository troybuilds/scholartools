Can I Still Pass If I Failed Two Tests?

Yes, many students can still pass a class after failing two tests, depending on how much the tests are worth and how many assignments remain.

Your final outcome depends on your overall average and performance on future coursework.

Things to Consider

  • The weight of the tests
  • Your current class average
  • Future assignments and exams
  • Extra credit opportunities

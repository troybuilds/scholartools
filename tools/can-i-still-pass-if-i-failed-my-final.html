Can I Still Get an A If I failed a test?

Yes — depending on how much the assignment or test is worth, you may still be able to get an A in the class.

A single failed test grade usually does not ruin your overall average, especially if:

  • You already had a high grade
  • The assignment was low weight
  • You still have future assignments or a final exam left

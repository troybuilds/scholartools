How Much Will a 65 Affect My Grade?

A 65 can lower your grade depending on your current average and how much the assignment is worth.

🎯 Example

  • Your current grade: 90%
  • The assignment is worth: 20%
  • You score: 65%

After this score, your grade would drop to about 85%.

📉 Why It Drops

A 65 is below most class averages, so it can pull your grade down, especially in weighted grading systems.

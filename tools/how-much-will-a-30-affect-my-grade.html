How Much Will a 30 Affect My Grade?

Receiving a 30% on an assignment can lower your overall grade, especially if the assignment is heavily weighted. The exact impact depends on your current grade and how much the assignment contributes to your final average.

Use the calculator below to estimate your new grade instantly.

🎯 Example

  • Your current grade: 88%
  • The assignment is worth: 20%
  • You score: 30%

After this score, your grade would drop to about 76%.

📉 Why It Drops

A 30% is well below average, so it pulls your grade down quickly, especially if it counts as a major test or project.

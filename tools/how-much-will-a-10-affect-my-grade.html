How Much Will a 10 Affect My Grade?

Receiving a 10% on an assignment can significantly lower your overall grade, especially if the assignment is heavily weighted. The exact impact depends on your current grade and how much the assignment contributes to your final average. Use the calculator below to estimate your new grade instantly.

Example

  • Your current grade: 88%
  • The assignment is worth: 20%
  • You score: 10%

After this score, your grade would drop to about 72%.

Why It Drops

A 10% is nearly a zero, so it has a major impact on your average when the assignment carries significant weight.

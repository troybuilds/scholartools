About ScholarTools

ScholarTools is a free educational website designed to help students understand their grades, GPA, and academic performance.

Our goal is to provide simple, accurate, and easy-to-use calculators that help students make informed decisions about their coursework.

Tools Available

  • Final Grade Calculator
  • Grade Calculator
  • Grade Percentage Calculator
  • Weighted Grade Calculator
  • GPA Calculator
  • College GPA Calculator
  • GPA Increase Calculator

ScholarTools also provides educational articles and grade-related resources to help students understand how grades are calculated and how different assignments affect academic performance.

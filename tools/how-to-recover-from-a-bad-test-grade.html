How to Recover From a Bad Test Grade

A bad test grade can feel discouraging, but one test usually does not determine your final grade.

Many students improve their averages by focusing on upcoming assignments and exams.

Ways to Improve Your Grade

  • Complete every remaining assignment
  • Prepare early for future tests
  • Ask about extra credit opportunities
  • Focus on heavily weighted categories

What Grade Do I Need to Get an A?

To get an A in your class, you need to calculate what score is required on your final exam based on your current grade and the weight of the final.

🎯 Example

Let’s say:

  • Your current grade: 85%
  • Your final exam is worth: 20%
  • You want an A (90%)

In this case, you would need to score about 115% on your final — which means it’s not possible without extra credit.

📘 Formula

The formula used is:

Required Grade = (Desired Grade - Current Grade × (1 - Final Weight)) ÷ Final Weight

💡 Tips to Get an A

  • Focus on high-weight assignments
  • Ask about extra credit opportunities
  • Study the most tested topics first

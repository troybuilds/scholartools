What Grade Do I Need to Get a 90?

To get a 90% in your class, you need to calculate what score is required on your final exam based on your current grade and the weight of the final.

🎯 Example

  • Your current grade: 85%
  • Your final exam is worth: 20%
  • You want a 90%

In this case, you would need to score about 115% on your final.

If your required grade is over 100%, it means you may need extra credit or it may not be possible to reach your goal.

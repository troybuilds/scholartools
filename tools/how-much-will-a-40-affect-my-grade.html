How Much Will a 40 Affect My Grade?

A 40% can have a significant impact on your overall grade depending on how much the assignment is worth. A small homework assignment may only lower your grade slightly, while a major exam could reduce your course average by several letter grades. Use the calculator below to find out exactly how much a 40% will affect your grade.

🎯 Example

  • Your current grade: 88%
  • The assignment is worth: 20%
  • You score: 50%

After this score, your grade would drop to about 80%.

📉 Why It Drops

A 40 is well below average, so it pulls your grade down quickly, especially if it counts as a major test or project.

If I Have a 90, What Will a 60 Do to My Grade?

If you currently have a 90% in your class, getting a 60 on an assignment can lower your grade depending on how much the assignment is worth.

🎯 Example

  • Your current grade: 90%
  • The assignment is worth: 20%
  • You score: 60%

After this score, your grade would drop to about 84%.

📉 What This Means

A drop from a 90 to an 84 can move you from an A to a B, depending on your grading scale. This shows how important each assignment can be.

Can I Still Get a B After a 0?

Yes, many students can still earn a B after receiving a 0, depending on how much the assignment was worth and how strong their grade was beforehand.

A single zero does not always ruin your final grade.

  • The assignment may have been low weight
  • You may have other grades to offset the impact
  • You may still have future tests and assignments remaining

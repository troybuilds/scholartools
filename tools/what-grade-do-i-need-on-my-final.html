What Grade Do I Need on My Final?

If you're wondering what grade you need on your final exam to reach a certain grade in your class, you're not alone. This is one of the most common questions students ask near the end of the semester.

Your final exam often makes up a large percentage of your overall grade, sometimes 20–40%. That means your performance on the final can significantly impact your final course grade.

How to Calculate What You Need on Your Final

To determine what score you need on your final exam, you need three numbers:

  • Your current grade in the class
  • The weight of the final exam
  • Your target grade for the course

Once you know those numbers, you can use the weighted grade formula to calculate the score required on your final.

Why Your Final Exam Matters

Because the final exam is usually one of the largest assignments in a course, it can dramatically change your final grade. A strong performance can boost your grade, while a poor score can lower it significantly.

That’s why many students check what they need on their final exam before the test so they know exactly what score to aim for.

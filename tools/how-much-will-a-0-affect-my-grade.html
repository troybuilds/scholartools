How Much Will a 0 Affect My Grade?

A 0 can have a major impact on your grade, especially if the assignment or test is worth a large percentage of your overall score.

🎯 Example

  • Your current grade: 90%
  • The assignment is worth: 20%
  • You score: 0%

After this score, your grade would drop to about 72%.

📉 Why a 0 Hurts So Much

A zero brings down your average more than any other score because it adds no points while still counting fully toward your total grade.

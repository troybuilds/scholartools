If I Have an 85, What Will a 70 Do to My Grade?

If you currently have an 85% in your class, getting a 70 on an assignment will lower your grade depending on how much that assignment is worth.

🎯 Example

  • Your current grade: 85%
  • The assignment is worth: 20%
  • You score: 70%

After this score, your grade would drop to about 82%.

📉 What This Means

A drop from an 85 to an 82 may not seem huge, but it can still affect your final grade depending on your class grading scale.

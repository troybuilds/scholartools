Is a 2.7 GPA Good?

A 2.7 GPA is slightly below a B average and represents mostly B- and C+ grades.

Is a 2.7 GPA Good for College?

Many colleges accept students with GPAs in the 3.0–3.5 range. However 2.7 GPA can still make you a competitive applicant at many state universities and private colleges.

Is a 2.7 GPA Good for Scholarships?

Some scholarships require GPAs of 3.0 or higher, however a 2.7 GPA may qualify you for certain merit scholarships.

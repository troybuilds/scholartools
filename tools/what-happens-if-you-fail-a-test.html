What Happens If You Fail a Test?

Failing a test can lower your class grade, but the exact impact depends on how much the test is worth and what your current grade is.

Many students are still able to recover from a failed test if they perform well on future assignments and exams.

Factors That Affect Your Grade

  • Your current grade
  • The test weight
  • Future assignments remaining
  • Extra credit opportunities

How to Raise Your Grade Fast

If you're trying to improve your grade quickly, focus on the assignments that have the biggest impact on your average.

  • Prioritize tests and major projects
  • Complete missing assignments
  • Study for upcoming exams
  • Ask your teacher about extra credit opportunities

Small improvements on heavily weighted assignments can raise your grade faster than many small homework scores.

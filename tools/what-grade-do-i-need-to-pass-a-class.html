What Grade Do I Need to Pass a Class?

Many students wonder what grade they need on their final exam or upcoming assignments to pass a class. The answer depends on your current grade and how much your final exam or remaining assignments are worth.

What Is a Passing Grade?

In most schools, a passing grade is usually:

  • 60% or higher
  • A letter grade of D or above

However, some schools or programs require a 70% or higher to pass.

How Final Exams Affect Your Grade

Final exams often count for a large portion of your total grade. Many finals are worth between 20% and 40% of the course grade.

This means your final exam score can significantly raise or lower your overall grade.

Example: Calculating the Grade You Need

Imagine the following situation:

  • Your current grade is 72%
  • Your final exam is worth 30% of the class
  • You need at least a 70% overall to pass

In this case, you may need around an 80% on your final exam to pass the class.

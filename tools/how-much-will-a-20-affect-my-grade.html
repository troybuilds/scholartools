How Much Will a 20 Affect My Grade?

A 20% is usually considered a failing score and can significantly reduce your overall grade if the assignment has a large weight. The exact change depends on your current average and the percentage the assignment is worth. Use the calculator below to calculate your updated grade.

🎯 Example

  • Your current grade: 88%
  • The assignment is worth: 20%
  • You score: 20%

After this score, your grade would drop to about 74%.

📉 Why It Drops

A 20 is well below average, so it pulls your grade down quickly, especially if it counts as a major test or project.

How to Calculate a Grade Percentage

Converting points to a grade percentage is a single division. Divide your points earned by the total points possible, then multiply by 100. That's your percentage grade.

Grade % = (Points Earned ÷ Points Possible) × 100

Example 1: 45 out of 50 points
Grade % = (45 ÷ 50) × 100 = 90% → A−

Example 2: 73 out of 100 points
Grade % = (73 ÷ 100) × 100 = 73% → C

Example 3: 17 out of 20 points
Grade % = (17 ÷ 20) × 100 = 85% → B

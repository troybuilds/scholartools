Is a 3.3 GPA Good?

A 3.3 GPA is generally considered above average and represents mostly B+ grades. It is a solid GPA that can make you competitive for many colleges and scholarships.

Is a 3.3 GPA Good for College?

Many colleges accept students with GPAs in the 3.0–3.5 range. A 3.3 GPA can make you a competitive applicant at many state universities and private colleges.

Is a 3.3 GPA Good for Scholarships?

Some scholarships require GPAs of 3.0 or higher, so a 3.3 GPA may qualify you for certain merit scholarships.

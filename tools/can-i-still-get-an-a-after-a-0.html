Can I Still Get an A After a 0?

Yes — depending on how much the assignment was worth, it may still be possible to keep an A in the class.

A zero can lower your grade quickly, but students often recover if:

  • The assignment had a low weight
  • You had a strong grade before
  • You still have quizzes, homework, or finals remaining

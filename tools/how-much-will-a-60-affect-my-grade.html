How Much Will a 60 Affect My Grade?

A 60 can significantly lower your grade, depending on how much the assignment or test is worth.

🎯 Example

  • Your current grade: 90%
  • The assignment is worth: 20%
  • You score: 60%

After this score, your grade would drop to about 84%.

📉 Why It Drops

Lower scores on high-weight assignments have a bigger impact on your overall grade. A 60 is significantly below most class averages, so it can pull your grade down quickly.

How Much Will a 50 Affect My Grade?

A 50 can lower your grade significantly, especially if the assignment has a high weight in your class.

🎯 Example

  • Your current grade: 88%
  • The assignment is worth: 20%
  • You score: 50%

After this score, your grade would drop to about 80%.

📉 Why It Drops

A 50 is well below average, so it pulls your grade down quickly, especially if it counts as a major test or project.

Why Did My Grade Drop After One Test?

Your grade may drop heavily after one test if the test category is weighted more than homework or participation assignments.

Many classes use weighted grading systems where tests and finals count for a large percentage of the overall grade.

  • Tests may be worth 40–70% of your class grade
  • A single low score can impact averages quickly
  • Future assignments can still help recover your grade
